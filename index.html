Sharing a memory of my late grandfather Jackson Ndlovu

l Bahle Ndhlovu a thirteen year old girl from Bulawayo would like to share a memory of a kind and honesty man like Jackson Ndlovu.

He was a man of his own words, who would just get happy for helping other people.He used to work at Gwanda Memorial Library. Many people loved him because he referd to them a donor who would give them food, clothes and many other things.

Besides providing food, he also paid school fees for orphans and those who were in need of help. He also helped many students by providing books and stationeries for them.He was also involved with several communinty development groups, including the Edward Ndlovu Memorial Trust, the Historical Association of Zimbabwe and the Mafela Trust.Many people still remember him for the good dedicated work that he had done.

Thank you all for taking your time to read about a kind man like Jackson Ndlovu
